Detailed Specifications:

  1. Processor:i7-8750H

    2. i7-8750H has a base clock speed of 2.2 GHZ and max turbo speed up to 4.0 GHZ

    It has 6 cores and 12 threads, and therefore multitasking will be a lot better

    Take note that not every 8th gen processors have 6 cores, definitely check the official documentation from https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/products/processors/core.html
  2. Graphics:NVIDIA Geforce GTX 1050 with Optimus

    3. The NVIDIA Geforce GTX 1050 can have a maximum dedicated memory of 4GB DDR5

    It's not the best value for money compared to the way stronger version: GTX 1050 TI, but it is enough for most games:

    • Dota 2 - 80 frames with highest graphics settings
    • CSGO - Totally no problem with minimal settings, but not the highest
    • Left 4 Dead 2 - The only issue that bothers me is the inconsistent connection, graphics? AWESOME
  3. RAM:8GB DDR4 RAM

    4. The RAVEN comes with a minimum of 8GB DDR4 RAM with a speed of 2400MHZ. Data transmission rate is expressed in MHZ

    There are two RAM slots in Illegear RAVEN. (One occupied)

    You can always add more RAM if you needed more.

  4. Storage:128GB SSD + 1TB HDD

    5. The RAVEN comes with two types of storage drives, The 2.5 inch 1TB Hitachi HDD and 128GB of M.2 PCIe NVMe Samsung SSD.

    There are two M.2 slots that is compatible with both SATA and NVMe SSDs.

    Go for SATA SSDs if budget is your main concern.

    Go for NVMe SSDs if disk read/write speed is your main concern.

  5. Operating system:Windows 10 Home

    6. Windows had the best compatibility with most games, due to its ubiquitous presence in computer manufacture industry.

    Not only that the OS is user-friendly, the built in softwares and utilities are useful as well.

  6. Price:RM4262

Review.

At here I will be expressing my personal feeling towards my machine.

This will include both my review and a more detailed look at the machine, Illegear RAVEN COFFELAKE

Update(4th August 2018): It had been a month. Everything works fine. Encountered some spiky ping issue, but fixable by using an ethernet cable. Will upload photoes sooner or later.

First impression ( How it all began )


I was recommended by one of my online singaporean friend with this brand.

The website is very user-friendly, considering that there is an online enquiry system that replied to me in a split second.
I was notified that the only Illegear Store is located at Oval Damansara, and it is not easy to reach there since I lived in Ampang.
But, I still managed to convince my dad to bring me there.

The personnel(Mostly Fazir) managed to answer my enquiries(and my dad's) with a calm manner despite the continuously repeated questions.
XD
First impression: Good customer service.

Overall Feeling ( Sypnosis of review )

1) The laptop is made of plastic entirely, but it is really sturdy.

2) The abundance of ports made me worry less about buying additional adapters, and therefore a happy customer.

3) The keyboard is comfortable to type on, and the lighting effects are superior, making the aesthetics even more complimentable.

4) I really liked some of the preinstalled software inside, they really gave me an enjoyable experience to modify some settings such as contrast and fan speed.

5) The manual fan turbo button makes the customizability of the laptop went up another level.

Review By Aspects

Hardware

The aesthetics and the solidity of Illegear RAVEN is out of expectation.

Ports

You will never need to worry about ports, the laptop had all of em! All you needed!

Keyboard

The GENESWITCH mechanical keyboard is one of the best keyboards I've ever typed on.

Software

Preinstalled softwares that grants you the access to modify every detail of your rig.

Accessories

Mouse and mousepad that enchanced my experience really deserves a clap.

Aftersales service

Excellent Customer service that satisfies me every single time.
Firstly, the screen. It comes with a 15.6" anti-glare screen, but the bezels are a bit thick.
The touchpad has a small light at the top left corner, and touching it can toggle the touchpad to be disabled or enabled.
The fan and vent works together pretty well, and when I use turbo cooling, my machine cools down fairly quick.
The lid can be opened with one hand due to its massive base.
I/O ports:
1 HDMI 2.0 output Port
1 USB 3.1 Gen. 1 Port (Type-C)
2 USB 3.1 Gen. 1 Ports
4 USB 2.0 Port
1 Headphone-out
1 Mic-in
1 RJ-45 LAN (10/100/1000Mbps)
1 DC-in Jack
All the ports are functional, except the USB 3.0s that is placed behind the machine will be annoying if u tend to lift you laptop with the backside as the pivot.
The keyboard layout is quite compact, but it does not affect my typing speed after some time of getting used to.
The lighting effects are superb. It is not some gimmick that can bore you overtime, it truly assists you to focus on your typing under dim enviroment.
Update(Aug 8th 2018): The spacebar of the laptop seems fragile, partly because of the supporting structure, a little bit of key movement flex is observed.
The primary software that aided me in every aspect when using this machine is:
Raven Command Center.
Not only that it allows you to manually modify the color and effects of your keyboard, but it also allows you to manually adjust the fan, screen color temperature, and turn off power source for display.
It monitors your cpu and gpu's temperature and changes the speed of the fan AUTOMATICALLY.
Another software worth mentioning should be the Fang Command Center
It allows you to modify some internal details of your mouse, including DPI and key bindings.

Mouse

 The Fang Gaming mouse is one of the free accessories that deserves my compliment. Not only that there are a bunch of bindable keys (7 programmable keys)(Like a usual gaming mouse)
BUT, It is all about the lighting effects!
The LED at the side of the buttons and the base animatable LED lights.
The scroll is smooth and functional, and everything else is right on the point.
Update(4th August 2018): The mouse's surface become smudgy easily if your hand tends to sweat profoundly, I think this is expected from a smooth surface.

MouseMat

 Another free accessory. The MatMaster is pretty huge(about 30cm*28cm) and it comes with two textures: Smooth and Coarse
The mat is too thick to be put between the lid and the keyboard, which means carrying it will require additional space.

Bag

 Free accessory too. The bag has an elegant appearance and the laptop fits inside the bag perfectly.(Guess this is pretty common huh)

Sleeve

 Free. It can provide additional protection to your laptop and can be used as cloth to clean your screen!!!

Other than being informative while I am making the decision whether or not to buy the laptop, they also did a good job after selling their laptop.
I received my laptop after two weeks of placing an order, which feels like the only experience that dissatisfies me. When I found that one of the softwares that is supposed to be installed on my machine went missing, I immediately contacted them via whatsapp. I carried my laptop into the store, and they fixed that for me in 10~15 mins.
Update(4th August 2018): I did not know that fn+f4 disables wi-fi, luckily I received aid from illegear, glad that I now know more about my laptop. :)
Conclusion (Updated on 4th August 2018): So far so good.
